<p><strong>The Automotive Torque Vectoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Torque Vectoring System (ATVS)</li><li>Passive Torque Vectoring System (PTVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive torque vectoring systems market is divided into two main types: Active Torque Vectoring Systems (ATVS) and Passive Torque Vectoring Systems (PTVS). ATVS dynamically adjusts the power distribution to individual wheels using electronic controls, enhancing traction and stability during various driving conditions. Conversely, PTVS relies on mechanical means, like differentials, to distribute torque based on wheel slip or road conditions, offering a simpler, more affordable solution. Both systems aim to improve vehicle performance and handling.</p></p>

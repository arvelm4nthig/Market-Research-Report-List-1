<p><p>Healthcare Decision Support Systems (HDSS) can be categorized into two main market types: stand-alone systems and integrated systems. Stand-alone systems operate independently, providing specific decision-making support, such as diagnostic assistance or treatment guidelines, often used by individual healthcare providers. In contrast, integrated systems are part of larger healthcare platforms, seamlessly interfacing with Electronic Health Records (EHR) and other healthcare applications. These integrated solutions enhance workflow efficiency and ensure comprehensive patient care by consolidating data and support tools within one cohesive system.</p></p>

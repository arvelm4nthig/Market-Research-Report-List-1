<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) transformers are essential for delivering power alongside data over Ethernet cables. The market comprises mainly two types: flyback and forward transformers. Flyback transformers are popular for isolating low-voltage devices from high-voltage sources, offering a compact design and simpler circuitry. In contrast, forward transformers provide higher efficiency and power transfer capability, making them suitable for applications requiring consistent output. Both types contribute to the growing demand for efficient and reliable PoE solutions in various sectors.</p></p>

<p><p>Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) transformers are crucial for supplying power to steel and metal production processes. The market for EAF transformers is categorized into two main types: AC furnace transformers and DC furnace transformers. AC furnace transformers provide alternating current to traditional EAF systems, ensuring efficient melting and refining of metals. In contrast, DC furnace transformers support direct current operations, which are often more energy-efficient and can enhance product quality. Both types are essential for optimizing steel production and energy consumption.</p></p>

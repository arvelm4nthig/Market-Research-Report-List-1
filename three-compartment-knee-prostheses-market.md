<p><p>The three-compartment knee prostheses market comprises three main types: fixed-bearing, mobile-bearing, and a combination of both. Fixed-bearing prostheses feature a stationary tibial component, providing stability and predictable outcomes but may limit natural knee motion. Mobile-bearing designs allow for rotational movement between components, enhancing knee function and reducing wear. The fixed or mobile-bearing market offers options that cater to various patient needs, balancing stability and mobility, ultimately influencing the choice based on individual anatomy and activity levels.</p></p>

<p><p>The urodynamic equipment market comprises various specialized devices used to evaluate urinary tract function. Uroflowmetry equipment measures urine flow rates. Cystometers assess bladder pressure and capacity. Ambulatory systems enable patient testing outside the clinic, while electromyographs evaluate pelvic floor muscle function. Video urodynamic systems combine imaging with functional testing for comprehensive analysis. Catheters facilitate fluid introduction into the bladder, and pumps & transducer sets support pressure monitoring during tests, collectively enhancing diagnosis and management of urological disorders.</p></p>

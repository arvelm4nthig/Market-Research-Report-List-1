<p><p>The trifocal intraocular lens (IOL) market includes hydrophobic and hydrophilic acrylic IOLs. Hydrophobic acrylic IOLs are designed to repel water, providing enhanced optical clarity and reduced glare, making them suitable for various lighting conditions. In contrast, hydrophilic acrylic IOLs absorb water, offering excellent biocompatibility and flexibility, which can aid in the surgical process. Both types aim to enhance vision at multiple distances, improving the quality of life for patients post-cataract surgery.</p></p>

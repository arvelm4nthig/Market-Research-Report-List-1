<p><strong>The Semiconductor CD SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photomask CD-SEM</li><li>Wafer CD-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor CD SEM market is divided into two main types: Photomask CD-SEM and Wafer CD-SEM. Photomask CD-SEM focuses on measuring critical dimensions of photomasks used in photolithography, ensuring precision before fabrication. In contrast, Wafer CD-SEM monitors the dimensions of structures directly on semiconductor wafers during or after manufacturing, crucial for process control and quality assurance. Both types are essential for maintaining the accuracy and reliability of semiconductor devices as technology advances toward smaller node sizes.</p></p>

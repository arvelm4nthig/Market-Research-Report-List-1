<p><strong>The Pleated Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MERV 7 and Below</li><li>MERV 8</li><li>MERV 9</li><li>MERV 10</li><li>MERV 11</li><li>MERV 12</li><li>MERV 13</li><li>MERV 14 and Above</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pleated filters are categorized based on their Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV), measuring their air filtration efficiency. MERV 7 and below capture larger particles, suitable for residential use. MERV 8 filters provide moderate filtration, targeting pet dander and dust. MERV 9 to 10 offer increased efficiency for allergens, while MERV 11 to 12 are effective against mold spores and finer dust. MERV 13 and above deliver superior filtration, ideal for commercial and healthcare environments, effectively trapping airborne pathogens and pollutants.</p></p>

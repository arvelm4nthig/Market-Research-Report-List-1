<p><p>The Quartz Cuvettes Market is divided into two main types: UV quartz cuvettes and IR quartz cuvettes. UV quartz cuvettes are designed for use in ultraviolet spectroscopy, offering high transmittance for UV light, making them ideal for analyzing absorbance in biological and chemical samples. In contrast, IR quartz cuvettes cater to infrared spectroscopy, providing stable performance for analyzing samples in the infrared wavelength range. Both types are essential for laboratory applications, ensuring precise measurements and accurate data collection across various scientific fields.</p></p>

<p><p>The automotive shock absorber rubber market comprises two primary types: natural rubber and chloroprene rubber. Natural rubber, derived from the latex of rubber trees, offers excellent elasticity and resilience, making it ideal for shock absorption applications. Chloroprene rubber, synthesized through polymerization, provides enhanced heat and weather resistance along with chemical stability, making it suitable for diverse environmental conditions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring vehicle comfort and performance by effectively dampening vibrations and impacts during operation.</p></p>

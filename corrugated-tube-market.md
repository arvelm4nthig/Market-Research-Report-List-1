<p><p>The corrugated tube market consists of two primary types: Single Wall Corrugated (SWC) and Double Wall Corrugated (DWC). SWC features a single layer of fluted material, providing lightweight and flexible solutions for various applications, such as packaging and industrial uses. In contrast, DWC consists of two layers, offering enhanced strength, insulation, and durability, making it ideal for construction, automotive, and heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to specific needs within diverse industries, emphasizing performance and versatility in design.</p></p>

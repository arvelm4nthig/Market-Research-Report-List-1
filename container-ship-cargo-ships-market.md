<p><p>Container ships are vessels designed to transport goods in standardized containers. The market is divided into two main types: FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) container ships. FCL ships carry containers booked by a single shipper, ideal for large cargo volumes, ensuring faster transit and reduced handling. LCL ships consolidate smaller shipments from multiple shippers into one container, offering cost-effective solutions for smaller cargo loads. Both types cater to diverse logistics needs, balancing efficiency, cost, and cargo volume requirements.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Infrared Gas Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point IR Detectors</li><li>Open Path IR Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared gas detector market includes two main types: point IR detectors and open path IR detectors. Point IR detectors are used for detecting gas in a specific location, featuring a small sensing area that targets emissions and provides localized monitoring. In contrast, open path IR detectors scan a wide area, utilizing beams of infrared light across a distance to identify gas leaks over larger spaces. Both types are essential for safety and environmental monitoring in various industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The reusable blood dialyzer market comprises three main types: hollow fiber, flat, and coil dialyzers. Hollow fiber dialyzers use numerous thin fibers to enhance surface area for effective filtration, making them the most common. Flat dialyzers consist of flat membranes and are suitable for specific applications due to their larger surface area but are bulkier. Coil dialyzers utilize a coiled design for maximum efficiency and compactness, providing effective blood purification while minimizing space requirements in medical settings.</p></p>

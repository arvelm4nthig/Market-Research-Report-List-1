Grease-filled PC strand incorporates lubrication to enhance durability and resistance against environmental factors, while wax-filled PC strand offers similar protection with different application methods for moisture control. Additional variants may include specialized coatings or treatments tailored for specific construction requirements, expanding the versatility of prestressed concrete applications.</p></p>

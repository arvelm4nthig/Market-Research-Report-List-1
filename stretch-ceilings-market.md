<p><p>Stretch ceilings are versatile architectural solutions that include two main market types: PVC ceilings and PTFE ceilings. PVC ceilings are made from polyvinyl chloride, offering a cost-effective, lightweight, and diverse range of finishes and colors, suitable for residential and commercial spaces. In contrast, PTFE ceilings, composed of polytetrafluoroethylene, provide superior durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, making them ideal for outdoor applications or environments requiring high performance. Both options enhance aesthetics while allowing innovative design possibilities.</p></p>

<p><p>The Bluetooth FM transmitter market is categorized into three main types based on power levels. Low power FM transmitters typically operate under 1 watt, suitable for short-range applications like personal use in vehicles. Medium power FM transmitters, ranging from 1 to 10 watts, serve broader applications, including small events and local broadcasts. High power FM transmitters exceed 10 watts and are designed for extensive coverage areas, often utilized in commercial broadcasting and larger public events, ensuring a stronger signal over longer distances.</p></p>

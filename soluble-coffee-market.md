<p><strong>The Soluble Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soluble coffee market features two primary types of processing: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly evaporating water from brewed coffee using hot air, resulting in fine powder granules. This method is efficient and cost-effective, producing instant coffee with a strong flavor. In contrast, freeze drying preserves flavor and aroma better by freezing brewed coffee and removing moisture under vacuum conditions. Freeze-dried coffee retains a more authentic taste and texture, appealing to premium segments of the market.</p></p>

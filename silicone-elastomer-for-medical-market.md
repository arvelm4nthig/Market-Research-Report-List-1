<p><strong>The Silicone Elastomer for Medical Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RTV</li><li>HTV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone elastomers for the medical market are categorized mainly into Room Temperature Vulcanized (RTV) and High-Temperature Vulcanized (HTV) silicone. RTV silicones cure at room temperature, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and ease of use. They are often utilized for molds, sealants, and medical devices. In contrast, HTV silicones are cured using heat, resulting in greater thermal stability and chemical resistance, suitable for more demanding applications like surgical instruments and implants. Both types ensure biocompatibility and safety for patient use.</p></p>

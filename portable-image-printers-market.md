<p><ul><li>Sublimation Printer</li><li>Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable image printer market primarily consists of two types: sublimation printers and inkjet printers. Sublimation printers use heat to transfer dye onto materials, producing vibrant, durable prints ideal for photographs. They offer quick, high-quality results and are often compact for easy transport. In contrast, inkjet printers spray liquid ink onto paper, providing versatility for various media and sizes. They excel in printing detailed images but may take longer to dry. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications.</p></p>

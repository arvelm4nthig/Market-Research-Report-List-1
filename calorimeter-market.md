<p><strong>The Calorimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Scanning Calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen Bomb Calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The calorimeter market consists of various types, primarily including Differential Scanning Calorimeters (DSC) and Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters. DSCs measure heat flow changes in materials as they are heated or cooled, providing insights into thermal properties and phase transitions. In contrast, Oxygen Bomb Calorimeters are designed to determine the heat of combustion of samples, playing a crucial role in energy content analysis. Together, these instruments serve vital functions in material science, pharmaceuticals, and energy research sectors.</p></p>

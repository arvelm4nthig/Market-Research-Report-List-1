<p><p>Bentonites are categorized into sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite based on their sodium and calcium content. Sodium bentonite is known for its swelling properties and is primarily used in applications such as drilling mud, cat litter, and sealants, due to its high absorption capacity. Conversely, calcium bentonite is less absorbent but has applications in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and as a binder in various industrial processes. The market dynamics for each type are driven by their varying properties and diverse industrial applications.</p></p>

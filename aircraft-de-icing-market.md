<p><p>The aircraft de-icing market consists of two primary types: de-icing trucks and de-icing fluids. De-icing trucks are specialized vehicles equipped with large tanks and spray systems to apply heated de-icing fluids directly onto aircraft surfaces, efficiently removing ice and snow. De-icing fluids are chemical solutions, typically glycol-based, designed to lower the freezing point of water and prevent ice accumulation on wings and surfaces. Together, these components ensure safe aircraft operation in cold weather conditions.</p></p>

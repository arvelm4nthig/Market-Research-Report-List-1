Hydraulic ramps utilize fluid pressure to lift heavy loads, offering stability and ease of use, making them ideal for heavy equipment. Pneumatic ramps, on the other hand, use compressed air for operation, providing a lightweight alternative suitable for moderate loads. Both types enhance productivity and safety during material handling operations, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

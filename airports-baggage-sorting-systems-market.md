<p><p>The **Airport Baggage Sorting Systems Market** includes **Tilt-tray** and **Cross-belt** systems. **Tilt-tray systems** use trays that tilt to drop bags onto designated chutes or conveyors, ideal for high-speed sorting with minimal damage. **Cross-belt systems** feature conveyor belts mounted on carts, allowing bags to be transferred laterally to sorting destinations, offering flexibility and precision. Both systems enhance efficiency, reduce manual handling, and cater to varying airport sizes and baggage volumes, ensuring smooth operations and improved passenger experience.</p></p>

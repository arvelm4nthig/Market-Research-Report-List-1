Nerve wraps provide support and protection to injured nerves, aiding healing by reducing scar tissue formation. Nerve grafts involve using a segment of healthy nerve tissue to replace the damaged portion, facilitating nerve regeneration. Together, these products are essential for treating peripheral nerve injuries and enhancing recovery outcomes.</p></p>

<p><p>The lubricating silicone grease market is categorized into three main types based on color and composition: white, gray, and black silicone grease. Gray silicone grease typically includes additional additives for enhanced performance in high-temperature and heavy-load environments. Black silicone grease, containing graphite or molybdenum, is ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring superior lubrication and protection against corrosion and wear.</p></p>

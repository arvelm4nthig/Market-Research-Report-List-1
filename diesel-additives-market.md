<p><p>The diesel additives market features several key types designed to enhance fuel performance. Cetane improvers boost ignition quality, improving combustion efficiency. Cold flow improvers prevent wax crystallization in low temperatures, ensuring smooth fuel flow. Lubricity improvers enhance the lubricating properties of diesel, protecting engine components. Pour point depressants (PPDs) lower the temperature at which diesel gels, facilitating easier use in cold conditions. Stabilizers help maintain fuel quality by preventing degradation and prolonging shelf life, ensuring optimal performance over time.</p></p>

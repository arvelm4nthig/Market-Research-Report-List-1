<p><strong>The Roof Guardrail System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-penetrating Guardrail System</li><li>Fixed Guardrail System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Roof Guardrail System market primarily features two types: Non-penetrating Guardrail Systems and Fixed Guardrail Systems. Non-penetrating systems are designed to secure roof edges without drilling into the surface, making them ideal for various roofing materials and allowing for easy installation and removal. Fixed Guardrail Systems, on the other hand, involve permanent installations that provide robust, long-term fall protection by being securely anchored to the roof. Both systems are essential for ensuring safety on rooftops during maintenance and construction.</p></p>

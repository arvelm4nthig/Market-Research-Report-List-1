<p><p>Green sand casting is a widely used metal casting process that employs moist sand as the mold material. This traditional technique involves a mixture of sand, clay, and water, which is compacted around a pattern to create a mold. Once the mold is formed, molten metal is poured into the cavity to produce desired shapes with high precision and good surface finish.</p><p>The Green Sand Casting Market is poised for substantial growth, driven by increasing demand in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.

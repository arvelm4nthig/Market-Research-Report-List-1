<p><ul><li>Passive Heat Sink</li><li>Active Heat Sink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum heat sinks are essential components for managing thermal dissipation in electronic devices. The market comprises two primary types: passive and active heat sinks. Passive heat sinks rely on natural convection and conduction to dissipate heat without additional energy input, often featuring fins or other structures to enhance airflow. In contrast, active heat sinks utilize fans or pumps to enhance cooling efficiency, actively moving air or fluids to reduce temperatures. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal performance in various applications.</p></p>

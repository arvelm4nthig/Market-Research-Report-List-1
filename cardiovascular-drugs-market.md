Diuretics promote fluid removal to reduce blood pressure and manage heart failure. Anti-clotting agents prevent thrombus formation, crucial for avoiding heart attacks and strokes. Antihyperlipidemic agents lower cholesterol levels, reducing cardiovascular disease risk through lipid regulation.</p></p>

<p><p>Mobile accounting apps can be categorized into three main types: On-Premise, Web-Based, and Cloud-Based. On-Premise apps are installed directly on a device and require local hardware for operation, providing full control but limited accessibility. Web-Based apps function through a browser, allowing real-time access from any internet-connected device without installation. Cloud-Based apps store data on remote servers, offering seamless updates, backup, and access from multiple devices, promoting flexibility and convenience for users and businesses.</p></p>

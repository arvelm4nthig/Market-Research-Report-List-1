<p><p>The high-calcium limestone market is segmented into three main types: light calcium carbonate, heavy calcium carbonate, and others. Light calcium carbonate is produced through a precipitation process, yielding fine, white particles with high purity, ideal for applications like food and pharmaceuticals. Heavy calcium carbonate, derived from grinding limestone, is commonly used in construction, paint, and plastics due to its larger particle size and greater opacity. The "others" category encompasses specialty products and various blends utilized in niche applications across diverse industries.</p></p>

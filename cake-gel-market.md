<p><strong>The Cake Gel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural</li><li>Synthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cake Gel market is categorized into two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural cake gels are derived from plant-based sources, offering clean labels and appealing to health-conscious consumers. They often contain ingredients like fruit pectin or agar-agar, enhancing moisture and texture without additives. Synthetic cake gels, on the other hand, are chemically formulated to provide consistency and stability in baked goods, often used by commercial bakers for their reliability and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to diverse baking needs and preferences.</p></p>

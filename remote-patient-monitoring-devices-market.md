<p><ul><li>Vital Sign Monitors</li><li>Blood Pressure Monitor</li><li>Pulse Oximeters</li><li>Heart Rate Monitor (ECG)</li><li>Temperature Monitor</li><li>Respiratory Rate Monitor</li><li>Brain Monitor (EEG)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) devices include various types that facilitate health tracking outside traditional clinical settings. Vital sign monitors assess essential health metrics, while blood pressure monitors track hypertension levels. Pulse oximeters measure oxygen saturation, and heart rate monitors (ECG) analyze cardiac activity. Temperature monitors detect fever, and respiratory rate monitors evaluate breathing patterns. Brain monitors (EEG) assess electrical activity in the brain. Other devices may include glucometers and wearable health trackers, collectively enhancing patient care and enabling proactive management of health conditions.</p></p>

<p><p>The Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) silicone rubber market is primarily segmented into two types: one-component and two-component silicones. One-component silicone is ready to use and cures upon exposure to moisture in the air, making it convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component silicone requires a specific mixture of base and curing agent, allowing for customizable properties and improved performance. Both types are widely utilized in construction, automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, catering to diverse bonding, sealing, and molding needs.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Portable Turbidity Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable turbidity analyzer market comprises two primary types: scattered light turbidimeters and transmitted light turbidimeters. Scattered light turbidimeters measure the intensity of light scattered by suspended particles at various angles, making them effective for detecting low turbidity levels in samples. In contrast, transmitted light turbidimeters measure the amount of light that passes through a sample, often suitable for higher turbidity ranges. Both types serve critical roles in water quality testing across industrial, environmental, and scientific applications.</p></p>

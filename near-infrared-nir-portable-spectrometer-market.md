<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) Portable Spectrometer market includes various types of spectroscopy techniques. Atomic Spectroscopy focuses on the analysis of elemental composition through light absorption or emission. Mass Spectrometry determines the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, useful in identifying chemical structures. Molecular Spectroscopy examines molecular interactions with light, providing insights into chemical bonds. Fluorescence Spectroscopy measures the light emitted from a sample after excitation, enabling detection of specific molecules. Together, these methods enhance NIR spectrometer applications across diverse industries.</p></p>

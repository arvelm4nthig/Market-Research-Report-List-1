<p><p>The Covid-19 antibody and serology test kit market comprises several types, including Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Chemiluminescent Immunoassays, and other methods. Chemiluminescent Immunoassays (CLIAs) offer high sensitivity and specificity, utilizing chemical reactions to detect antibodies in the lab. Other market types may include enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and multiplex tests, catering to varied needs for antibody detection and screening in different settings.</p></p>

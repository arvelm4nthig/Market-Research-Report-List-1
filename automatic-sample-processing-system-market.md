<p><p>The Automatic Sample Processing System market consists of two primary types: fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic systems perform all sample processing tasks autonomously, minimizing human intervention and enhancing efficiency, precision, and throughput. In contrast, semi-automatic systems require some manual input or oversight during the processing stages, offering a balance between automation and user control. Both types cater to varying laboratory needs, with fully automatic systems favored for high-volume environments and semi-automatic systems preferred for flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>

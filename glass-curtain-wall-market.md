<p><p>The glass curtain wall market features three primary types: single glazed, double glazed, and triple glazed. Single glazed systems offer basic insulation and are cost-effective but less energy-efficient. Double glazed units enhance thermal performance, using two glass layers with an insulating space, making them popular for modern buildings. Triple glazed types provide superior insulation and energy efficiency, featuring three glass panes, ideal for extreme climates. Each type addresses varying aesthetic, performance, and energy efficiency needs in architectural design.</p></p>

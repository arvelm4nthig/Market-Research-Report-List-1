<p><strong>The Electric Two Way Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Acting Electric Two Way Valve</li><li>Pilot Operated Electric Two Way Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Two Way Valve market is primarily categorized into two types: Direct Acting Electric Two Way Valves and Pilot Operated Electric Two Way Valves. Direct Acting Electric Two Way Valves use an electric actuator to directly control the valve position, providing quick response and precise control, ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, Pilot Operated Electric Two Way Valves employ a pilot valve to control the main valve, allowing for larger flow capacities and higher pressures, making them suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>

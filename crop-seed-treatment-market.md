<p><p>The crop seed treatment market offers three primary types: liquid, granular, and powder. Liquid treatments are applied directly to seeds, ensuring an even coating that enhances protection and nutrient uptake. Granular treatments consist of dry formulations that adhere to the seed surface, providing long-lasting protection against pests and diseases. Powder treatments are finely milled substances that can be dusted onto seeds, offering versatility in application and targeted protection. Each type serves specific agricultural needs, enhancing seed performance and yield potential.</p></p>

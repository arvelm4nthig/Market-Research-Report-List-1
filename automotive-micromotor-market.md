<p><strong>The Automotive Micromotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed DC Motor</li><li>Brushless DC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Micromotor market includes Brushed DC Motors and Brushless DC Motors, both essential for vehicle applications. Brushed DC Motors use brushes for commutation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with limited efficiency and lifespan due to wear. Brushless DC Motors, on the other hand, use electronic commutation, providing higher efficiency, durability, and precise control, making them ideal for advanced automotive systems like electric power steering and HVAC. While brushed motors are common in basic functions, brushless motors dominate in modern, high-performance automotive applications.</p></p>

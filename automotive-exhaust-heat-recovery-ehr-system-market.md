<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery (EHR) System market includes several technologies. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) captures waste heat from exhaust to enhance engine efficiency. Rankine Cycle Systems convert heat into mechanical energy using a working fluid, improving fuel economy. Thermoelectric Generators generate electricity directly from temperature differences, offering seamless integration with vehicle systems. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) recovers energy from exhaust gases to assist the engine, enhancing performance and reducing fuel consumption. Each contributes to efficient energy utilization in vehicles.</p></p>

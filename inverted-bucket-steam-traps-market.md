<p><p>Inverted bucket steam traps are essential devices used in steam systems to efficiently remove condensate while maintaining low steam loss. They operate on the principle of buoyancy, with a bucket that rises when steam is present and sinks when condensate accumulates, allowing for efficient drainage. These traps are widely used in various industries, including food processing, power generation, and chemical manufacturing, where precise temperature control and efficient energy use are crucial.</p><p>The Inverted Bucket Steam Traps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.

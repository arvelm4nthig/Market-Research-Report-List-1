<p><p>Earthing transformers, or neutral couplers, are essential for grounding electrical systems, providing a reference point for voltage stabilization and fault current management. The market is primarily divided into two types: oil-type and dry-type transformers. Oil-type transformers use insulating oil for cooling and insulation, offering higher capacity and efficiency, suitable for larger installations. In contrast, dry-type transformers employ air or resin for insulation, providing safer operation in indoor environments with lower maintenance needs and reduced fire risk, making them ideal for smaller applications.</p></p>

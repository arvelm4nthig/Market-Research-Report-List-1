<p><p>The vacuum bagging material market consists of several key types. Vacuum bagging film is the primary layer that creates a sealed environment, ensuring optimal pressure during the curing process. Release film prevents adhesion between layers, facilitating easy demolding. Peel ply offers a textured surface for bonding, while breather/bleeder materials allow air and excess resin to escape during vacuuming, ensuring even compaction. Other products in this market include sealants and adhesive films that support various composite manufacturing processes.</p></p>

<p><p>Heterojunction (HJT) solar panels are advanced photovoltaic technology that combines thin layers of amorphous silicon with crystalline silicon to create a structure that enhances energy conversion efficiency. This design minimizes electron recombination and improves overall performance, particularly under low-light conditions.

<p><p>The automotive dryer market includes various types designed for efficient drying processes. **Rotary dryers** use rotating drums to dry materials uniformly, ideal for bulk drying. **Dispersion dryers** employ high-speed airflow to disperse and dry materials quickly, suitable for fine particles. **Fluid bed dryers** utilize a fluidized bed of air to dry materials evenly, often used for heat-sensitive products. **Belt dryers** feature conveyor belts for continuous drying, perfect for large-scale operations. **Others** include specialized dryers tailored for unique automotive drying needs, ensuring versatility and efficiency.</p></p>

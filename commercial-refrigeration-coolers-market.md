<p><p>The commercial refrigeration coolers market includes various types based on refrigerants and cooling systems. NH3 (ammonia) systems are known for their energy efficiency and effectiveness in large-scale applications. CO2 systems leverage carbon dioxide as a refrigerant, offering low environmental impact. Glycol systems use a water-glycol mixture for cooling applications, particularly in food processing. Other systems may include alternative refrigerants and solenoid-operated cooling technologies.</p></p>

<p><p>The handheld salt meter market features three main types: Optical, Conductivity, and Hydrometer. Optical types use light absorption to measure salinity, offering quick and non-destructive assessments. Conductivity types gauge the electrical conductivity of a solution, providing accurate salt concentration readings. Hydrometer types utilize buoyancy principles to assess salt content by measuring density changes in the solution. Each type serves specific applications, catering to industries such as agriculture, food, and environmental monitoring, emphasizing portability and ease of use.</p></p>

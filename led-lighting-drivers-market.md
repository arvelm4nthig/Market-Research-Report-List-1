<p><ul><li>DALI</li><li>0-10V Dimming</li><li>Standard(non-dim)</li><li>Triac Dimming</li><li>Smart Driver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED lighting drivers market comprises several key types. DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) allows for advanced control and monitoring of lighting systems. 0-10V dimming enables smooth light level adjustments through a simple voltage signal. Standard (non-dim) drivers provide fixed output without dimming functionality for straightforward applications. Triac dimming uses phase-cut technology to adjust brightness with existing dimmer switches. Smart drivers incorporate wireless connectivity and automation features, enabling integration with smart home systems for enhanced control and energy management.</p></p>

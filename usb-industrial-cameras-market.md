<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The USB industrial cameras market is primarily segmented into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for applications requiring high sensitivity and precision. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer higher speed, lower power consumption, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for real-time imaging applications. Both technologies cater to various industries, including manufacturing, robotics, and medical imaging, fulfilling diverse operational needs.</p></p>

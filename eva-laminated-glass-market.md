<p><p>EVA laminated glass is a type of safety glass made by sandwiching a layer of ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA) film between two or more layers of glass. The EVA film is thermally bonded to the glass layers under heat and pressure, creating a durable, transparent, and impact-resistant material. This glass is widely used in applications such as architectural glazing, automotive windshields, solar panels, and decorative glass due to its superior strength, UV resistance, and sound insulation properties.</p><p>The EVA laminated glass market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for safety glass in construction, automotive, and renewable energy sectors. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.5% during the forecast period. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and the Middle East are also driving growth due to rapid industrialization and infrastructure development.</p></p>

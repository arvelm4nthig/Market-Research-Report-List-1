<p><p>The automotive adjustable steering system market is categorized primarily into two types: manually adjustable steering and electrically adjustable steering. Manually adjustable steering allows drivers to change the steering wheel's position using physical controls, enhancing comfort and driving ergonomics. In contrast, electrically adjustable steering systems utilize electric motors to automatically adjust the steering wheel's position, offering greater convenience and precision. This technology often integrates with advanced driver-assistance systems, providing enhanced adaptability and user experience in modern vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>The copper foil market for wireless charging consists of two primary types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process, resulting in a thin, uniform sheet that offers excellent conductivity and is ideal for high-performance applications. Rolled copper foil, on the other hand, is created by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, providing robust characteristics and higher tensile strength. Both types serve essential roles in manufacturing coil components for efficient wireless charging solutions.</p></p>

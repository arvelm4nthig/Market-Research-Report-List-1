<p><p>The LED Emergency Battery Pack market primarily features two types of battery packs: Sealed Lead-Acid Batteries and Nickel-Cadmium Batteries. Sealed Lead-Acid Batteries are known for their robustness, long life, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Nickel-Cadmium Batteries offer a larger discharge capacity and exceptional performance in extreme temperatures, although they are more expensive and have environmental concerns due to cadmium content. Both types serve critical roles in providing reliable emergency lighting solutions.</p></p>

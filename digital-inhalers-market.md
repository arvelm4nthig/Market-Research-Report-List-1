<p><p>The digital inhalers market encompasses two primary types: Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs). MDIs deliver a pre-measured dose of medication in aerosol form, often integrating smart technology for tracking usage and ensuring proper inhalation technique. DPIs, on the other hand, provide medication in powdered form, requiring patients to inhale forcefully to receive their dose, with digital features that monitor usage and adherence. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes through improved medication management.</p></p>

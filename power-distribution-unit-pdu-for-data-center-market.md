<p><p>Power Distribution Units (PDUs) in data centers come in various types, each serving distinct functions. Basic PDUs simply distribute power without advanced features. Metering PDUs provide basic power consumption measurement, allowing for tracking energy usage. Monitoring PDUs offer more in-depth analysis, enabling real-time monitoring of power metrics and alerts. Switch PDUs add further control by allowing remote power on/off capabilities for connected devices. Together, these PDUs enhance efficiency, management, and reliability in data center power distribution.</p></p>

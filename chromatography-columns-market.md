<p><p>The chromatography columns market comprises three key types: High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) columns, Gas Chromatographic (GC) columns, and Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) columns. HPLC columns are designed for analyzing liquid samples with high precision, often used in pharmaceuticals and environmental studies. GC columns handle gaseous samples, typically for volatile compounds in various industrial applications. SPE columns facilitate the extraction and purification of analytes from complex mixtures, commonly employed in biological and environmental sample preparations, enhancing the accuracy of analytical results.</p></p>

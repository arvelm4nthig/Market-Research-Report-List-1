<p><ul><li>AC Electric Traction Transformer</li><li>DC Electric Traction Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Traction Transformer market primarily consists of two types: AC Electric Traction Transformers and DC Electric Traction Transformers. AC transformers are utilized in alternating current systems, typically for high-speed rail and urban transit, functioning to step down voltage for efficient power distribution. In contrast, DC transformers serve direct current applications, often found in metro systems and electric freight trains, converting high voltages for traction power. Both types are critical for optimizing energy delivery and performance in rail transport.</p></p>
